Culture

Salone Culture

The majority of people in Sierra Leone still live a traditional, agricultural way of life, with ruling chiefs, and religions which preserve social stability, as well as local music, dance, customs and traditions. Handshaking is the normal form of greeting. It is usual to be entertained in a hotel or resturant, particular for business visitors. Small tokens of appreciation is always welcome. Casual wear are sutable everywhere, men are rarely expected to wear suits and ties.
